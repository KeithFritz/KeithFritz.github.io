Hobbies and Interests

A brief look into my personal life and interests.

Travel and Enjoy Life

When I can, I love to travel. Engaging with diverse cultures and connecting with people from different backgrounds enriches my travel experiences, leaving me with profound insights and cherished memories. While I do enjoy eating new foods wherever I go, my true passion lies in the exploration of new landscapes, engaging in thrilling activities, and creating unforgettable moments with friends. I love to learn about all the world has to offer and broaden my horizons as much as I can.

Making

In my personal time, I still love making things. Generally, that takes the form of making things out of wood from building a desk for my house to making a cutting board for a friend. I love the process of taking something in a raw form and turning it into something useful and beautiful. I also love the process of learning new skills which is why I try to learn something new everything I make something. I find that this love for learning and making has been especially useful in my career as an engineer. I am always looking for new ways to solve problems and learn new skills to help me do that.

If you're interested in getting to know more about me, feel free to email me at keith.fritz@me.com
