Discover Keith Fritz

Welcome to my portfolio! I’m Keith Fritz, an Austin, Texas-based engineer and designer with a passion for creating innovative and efficient solutions. My expertise spans robotics, system design, embedded systems, hardware development, and rapid prototyping. With a career focused on blending creative design with practical engineering, I strive to tackle complex challenges and deliver systems that are both technically sound and thoughtfully designed. Explore my work to see how I turn ideas into reality through a combination of technical precision, adaptability, and a love for problem-solving.

I recently worked at ARIS Technology in Chicago, where I designed, built, and delivered large-scale industrial turnkey 3D scanning robotic cells. These systems have been successfully integrated into automotive manufacturing plants, military manufacturing facilities, and other high-demand environments. Prior to that, I worked with Texas Robotics in Austin, where I supported researchers by developing and managing a variety of robotic research platforms. I also oversaw rapid manufacturing facilities, helping to ensure the efficient development and testing of cutting-edge robotic systems.
